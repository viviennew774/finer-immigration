FI Employer Sponsor Visa Specialists - What should I consider before hiring a Working Holiday Maker ?

  • By Vivienne
  • at December 12, 2018

What should I consider before hiring a Working Holiday Maker? Work should not be the main purpose of the visa-holder’s stay. Any work should be secondary to the main purpose of the stay — a holiday. The visa holder can only work with the same employer for a maximum of six months. The Working Holiday Maker may have extra visa conditions and a breach of any condition could result in the visa being cancelled and the visa holder having to leave Australia.
