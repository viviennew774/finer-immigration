Migration Law and Practice in Melbourne

Australian Visa is not one-size fits-all, it is important that you find the right one for the employee to suit your company structure. You can employ workers who are living overseas but want to come work in Australia by either Sponsoring skilled workers on a permanent basis through the Employer Nomination Scheme or the Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme. You also can sponsoring a worker on a temporary basis through the Temporary Work Skilled subclass 457 visa ( subclass 482) , which allows them to come to Australia to work for up to 4 years. You can also enter into a Labour agreement with the Australian Government. More about the options
